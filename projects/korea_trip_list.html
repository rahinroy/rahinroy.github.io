Shopping · Busan — Department Store
신세계 센텀시티 (Shinsegae Centum City)
📍 Centum City, Busan
The world's largest department store. It's basically a small city: luxury shopping, Spaland spa (best spa in Busan per locals), ice rink, cinema, restaurants, cafés. Easily a full day. Right in Busan — a must if you're visiting.

Food Hall · Gangnam — Korea's Largest NEW
신세계 강남 프리미엄 델리 (Shinsegae Gangnam Premium Deli)
📍 B1F, 176, Sinbanpo-ro, Seocho-gu, Seoul (connected to Goto Mall / Express Bus Terminal)
Korea's largest food hall at 42,700 sq ft. Features a famous Japanese onigiri shop (1-hour queues in Japan, Korea's first!), chefs from top fine dining programs, plus Singaporean, Thai, Vietnamese, and more. Mon–Thu 10:30–20:00 / Fri–Sun 10:30–20:30.

📍 Seoul (Hongdae / Myeongdong / Seongsu area)
