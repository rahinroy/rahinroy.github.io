Mortgage interest deduction on up to $750k of loan principal. Tax savings calculated as the incremental federal tax reduction from buying a house. If your CA state income tax alone exceeds the standard deduction, the baseline assumes you're already itemizing with SALT — only the additional benefit from mortgage interest and property tax is counted. CA state tax estimated using 2025 brackets. Use the Mortgage Year slider to see how the interest/principal split and tax benefit change over the life of the loan. Rent vs buy comparison: the renter invests monthly savings (buy net cost − rent) at the risk-free rate, compounding monthly over the comparison horizon.
